## Biological Preparedness

- **Behaviorists assumed**: any NS can be paired with any US
- **Reality**: Organisms are biologically prepared to form **certain associations more easily than others**

| Easy to Associate (mammals) | Hard to Associate (mammals) |
|----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Novel taste (NS) + nausea (CR) | Novel taste (NS) + electric shock (CR) |
| Flash of light (NS) + electric shock (CR) | Flash of light (NS) + nausea (CR) |

> [!tip] Why?
> Taste is a reliable signal for **food safety** in mammals. Birds use vision instead — so birds more easily associate visual cues with illness.

---

## Conditioned Taste Aversion (Garcia Effect)

- Taste of a food (NS) + illness (UR) → food becomes CS → nausea (CR)
- **Violates standard acquisition rules**:

| Standard Rule | CTA Exception |
|---------------|---------------|
| Many pairings needed | **ONE pairing** is enough |
| CS must be close in time to US | **Hours can elapse** |
| Any modality works | **Only taste**; not light or sound + nausea |

### Example: Chemotherapy patients
- Children receiving chemo develop aversions to food eaten just before treatment (e.g., hospital ice cream)
- **"Diet scapegoat"**: A special novel food given *before* chemo acts as the scapegoat CS — the child develops aversion to the scapegoat, protecting their normal foods

## Applications of Classical Conditioning

| Application | How It Works |
|-------------|-------------|
| Phobias | NS (dog) + US (bite) → CS (dog) → CR (fear) |
| Little Albert | Demonstrated human fear conditioning (Watson & Rayner) |
| Wildlife control | Aversion conditioning (e.g., coyotes conditioned to avoid sheep via taste aversion) |
| Chemotherapy | Diet scapegoat to weaken sweet taste-calorie association with no-calorie sweeteners |

---

## Exam/Test Patterns

| Scenario/Keyword | Answer |
|-----------------|--------|
| Single pairing caused taste aversion | **CTA exception** — one trial is sufficient |
| Nausea 3 hours after eating → aversion to that food | **CTA** — long delay still works |
| Why doesn't music cause nausea in chemo patients? | **Preparedness** — mammals link taste (not sound) with illness |
| Hospital ice cream aversion: is US the drug or food? | **US = drug**; **NS/CS = ice cream** |
