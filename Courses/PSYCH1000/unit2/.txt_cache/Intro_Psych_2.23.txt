1. Unconditioned stimulus (US)
À Stimulus that naturally and automatically triggers unconditioned response (UR)
2. Unconditioned response (UR)
À Naturally occurring response to unconditioned stimulus (US)
3. Neutral stimulus (NS)
À Stimulus that elicits no response before conditioning
4. Conditioned stimulus (CS)
À Originally neutral stimulus that, after association with unconditioned stimulus (US), comes to trigger conditioned response (CR)
5. Conditioned response (CR)
À Learned response to previously neutral (but now conditioned) stimulus (CS)
